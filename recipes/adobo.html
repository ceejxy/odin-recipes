Adobo Recipe

Description

Adobo is a dish that is originated in the Philippines. It had a lot of Spanish influence. It is a chicken dish, although it may sometimes be made with pork or fish. It has a sweet and savory taste. It is also very easy to make!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Soy Sauce
  • Brown Sugar
  • White Onion
  • White Vinegar
  • Black Peppercorn
  • Bay Leaves
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Season chicken
  2. Cut onions and garlic
  3. Add water to put and boil chicken, onions, and garlic together
  4. Add in soy sauce and vinegar
  5. Add sugar to taste
  6. Add in bay leaves and peppercorns
  7. Simmer until chicken is tender and sauce becomes thicker
  8. Serve and enjoy!
