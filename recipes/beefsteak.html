Beef Steak Recipe

Description

Beef Steak, also known as Bistek is another Filipino dish that has alot of Spanish influence. It is a beef dish that is marinated in soy sauce and lemon, giving it a savory, salty taste. It is best eaten with rice. It is another delicious meal that is easy to both prepare make!

Ingredients

  • Thinly Sliced Beef
  • Soy Sauce
  • Red Onion
  • Lemon
  • Black Pepper
  • Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Create marinade using soy sauce, lemon, black pepper and garlic powder
  2. Cut beef into thin slices and put the beef in the marinade
  3. Let beef marinate for 8+ hours, overnight for best flavor
  4. Cook beef with the marinade until the marinade becomes a thicker sauce
  5. Serve and enjoy!
