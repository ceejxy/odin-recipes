Chicken Katsu Recipe

Description

Chicken Katsu is a Japanese dish that consists of chicken breast, rice, and assorted vegetables. The chicken is fried in panko and served with a yummy sauce.

This delicious dish is easy to make and will surely impress your guests or anyone you make it for!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Green Onion
  • Avocado
  • Jasmine Rice
  • Cucumber
  • Mayonaise
  • Siracha
  • Panko Bread Crumbs
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Soy Sauce
  • Honey
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Flour
